Initiatives that support Pc

Green Manufacturing

-Energy-efficient Components: Manufacturers produce energy-efficient CPUs, GPUs, and power supplies (e.g., ENERGY STAR-certified devices).

-Sustainable Materials: Using recycled or biodegradable materials in hardware.

Energy-efficient Usage

Power Management: Operating systems provide settings like sleep mode, hibernation, and automatic power-off to save energy.

E-Waste Management

Recycling Programs: Organizations like E-Stewards and R2 Certified Recyclers ensure proper disposal and recycling of electronic waste.
Refurbishment Initiatives: Charities and companies refurbish old PCs for reuse (e.g., Computer Aid International).
